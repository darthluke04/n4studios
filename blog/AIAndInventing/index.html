AI And Inventing

My opinion on the Growth of AI and Inventions

Artificial Inteligence (AI) is growing fast and it is not going to stop growing until it doesnt have anywhere to go. In some cases thats good, because we get new ways to do things and new inventions. But in some ways that is bad because if we invent things without thinking about the possibility of the invention getting into the wrong hands things eventualy will get out of hand.

History and movies show what happens. "Mission Impossible: Dead Reckoning (2023)"Z, "I, Robot (2004)", "Happy Death Day 2U (2019)", "The Terminator (1984)", "War Games (1983)", "WALL-E (2008)". I could go on.

If we make the AI more secure so that it cannot be controlled, it WILL get out of hand. Now I know nothing is fully secure but just because nothing is fully secure, that does not mean we should just ignore the fact that we should still try.
